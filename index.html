About Me

Hi - Nice to meet ya! My name is Garrett Steed and I would invite you to read on to learn more about who I am.

As I child, I wanted to fly fighter jets but now I have a phobia of flying and sell software. Weird how life works right? I currently work as an Inside Account Manager at Adobe but I have held quite a few jobs over the years. Some of these include working for the EPA, being on a chainsaw crew, and researching juaguars in the jungles of Costa Rica.

Outside of work I enjoy to read, watch movies and anime, and play videogames. Many of these hobbies got lost in the flow of life right now but the one new hobby I have kept with is climbing. It is enjoyable and challenging, ecpescially for me. I generally don't fit the climbers "frame", as I am a 6' 2 240lb corn feed Ohian. Either way, it has been a great thing to do and has kept me sane through the pandemic.
